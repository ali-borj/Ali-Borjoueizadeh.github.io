Research Philosophy

The core of my work revolves around the Digital Curator mindset—not just analyzing data, but contextualizing it within the human experience. My research explores how Large Language Models (LLMs) can be utilized to mirror complex human emotional states and their subsequent impact on systemic structures like global markets.

By integrating emotion modeling into computational frameworks, I aim to develop more empathetic and accurate predictive indicators. This multidisciplinary approach ensures that technology remains grounded in the nuances of human psychology.
